About Us

BuzzGames is a review website project started in 2017. Our mission is to present to the users a diverse content on videogames and their growing presence in society. We believe in the philosophy of elevating the appreciation and understanding of different categories of games. All reviews available on the website are not judged purely on fun, entertainment value, or technical craftsmanship. The cultural and creative contexts are also considered. The numerical ratings are based on the game's merits.
